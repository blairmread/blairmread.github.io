About me

I am a fourth-year PhD candidate in the Political Science Department at MIT. I study policy-making in the Global South, focusing on service delivery and the interplay between the state and its non-state counterparts.

I received my BA from Tufts University in 2013. Prior to graduate school, I work as a Research Support Associate in the Political Science Department at MIT, and a field research coordinator for MIT GOV/LAB in Tanzania and Uganda.
